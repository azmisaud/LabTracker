Department of Computer Science

Aligarh Muslim University

About the Department

The Department of Computer Science at Aligarh Muslim University stands as a beacon of excellence in computer science education and research in India. Established in 1971 with the installation of the IBM1130 Computing System, our department has evolved into a premier institution recognized by prestigious ranking agencies including Times Higher Education, India Today, and NIRF.

Our journey of innovation began with the establishment of the Computer Centre, which later blossomed into an independent department in 1988. A significant milestone was achieved in 1980 with the installation of the VAX11/780 system from DEC (USA), marking one of the first such installations in India.

The department's academic evolution reached new heights in 1982 with the UGC's approval of two pioneering programs: the three-year Master of Computer Science and Applications (MCA) and the one-year Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Science Applications (PGDCA), which commenced in the 1983-84 academic session.

Today, we offer a comprehensive range of programs including Ph.D., M.C.A., M.Sc. in Cyber Security & Digital Forensics, P.G.E.D.P., P.G.D.C.P., and B.Sc. (Hons.) Computer Applications. Our curriculum is meticulously designed to meet contemporary industry demands while fostering innovation and research.

The department's strength lies in its distinguished faculty, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and industry partnerships. Our facilities include modern conference rooms, smart classrooms, and dedicated computing laboratories for research scholars and students at all levels.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our students' success. Graduates from our department are highly sought after by leading IT companies such as TCS, Accenture, Infosys, IBM, HCL, Wipro, and many others. The department's placement record is exemplary, with students securing positions even before completing their final semesters.

Vision

To achieve global recognition in computer science education, research, and training while addressing the evolving needs of industry and society.
